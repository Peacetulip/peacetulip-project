Peacetulip.org focuses on the guiding values of social entrepreneurship.

It seeks to connect with like-minded non-profit organizations and thinkers around the world.


- Serve the economy in the name of justice
- Embrace cultural differences
- Seek harmony between religions and philosophies
- Explore ways to pursue meaningful happiness
- Education as Empowerment
- Stewardship of the Earth


Peacetulip.org is also Inspired by Practice

Peacetulip.org provides examples of how products and services can be designed to align with our guiding values. These examples are entirely for educational purposes. The goal is to inspire others and demonstrate how social entrepreneurship can work in practice. Even if only thought of as an inspiration or a thought experiment.
