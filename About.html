About


Peacetulip is an educational initiative that explores how to create social enterprises rooted in:

justice,
cultural understanding
spiritual and philosophical harmony
education as empowerment and
sustainance and environment.

Our goal is not to offer ready-made solutions, but to invite exploration:
What does it mean to build a business that serves the common good and not just in one country, but across cultures and belief systems?
Through Peacetulip.org we provide a framework and inspiration to those who want to walk this path. This is a slowly unfolding, open-source journey, one that grows with every contribution. We welcome thinkers, doers, and dreamers who wish to contribute.
