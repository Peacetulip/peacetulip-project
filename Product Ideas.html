Product Ideas


Inspired by Our Guiding Values: Project & Product Ideas

Economy in Service of Justice

  • Ethical Investment Platform: A digital space where users can support local or global projects that prioritize fairness, dignity, and sustainability over profit.
  • Fair Trade Discovery App: A mobile app mapping businesses with transparent, ethical supply chains.
  • Economic Justice Simulation Game: A game where players design economies centered on equity, cooperation, and long-term wellbeing.

Embracing, Not Fighting, Cultural Differences

  • Cross-Cultural Dialogue Cards: Conversation prompts for workshops and classrooms to explore diverse customs and worldviews.
  • Intercultural Cooking Series: Recipes paired with cultural stories to foster appreciation and connection.
  • Video/Podcast Series: Real conversations between people of diverse backgrounds focused on learning, not judging.

Religious and Philosophical Harmony

  • Shared Wisdom Calendar: An inclusive calendar with key teachings and dates from major traditions.
  • Interfaith Conversation App: A respectful digital space to ask questions and share insights across spiritual traditions.
  • Mini-Books on Worldviews: Concise, accessible texts introducing diverse traditions with an emphasis on harmony.

Happiness

  • Reflective Journal or App: A space to track meaningful moments, gratitude, and life beyond consumption.
  • “Walks of Meaning” Card Set: Mindful walk prompts to deepen awareness, gratitude, and connection.
  • Workshop Series: “Personal Happiness, Collective Wellbeing” — linking inner peace with social good.

Education as Empowerment

  • Empowerment Toolkits: Resources on rights, critical thinking, and financial literacy — especially for underserved groups.
  • Story Platform: Personal testimonies on how education has transformed lives.
  • Intergenerational Mentorship App: Connecting learners and mentors across backgrounds for mutual learning.

Ecological Responsibility

  • Conscious Living App: Personalized tips for small, impactful lifestyle changes.
  • Modular, Repairable Products: Designed for longevity and sustainability.
  • Nature as Teacher Curriculum: Modules for schools or self-learners combining science, ecology, and wisdom traditions.
